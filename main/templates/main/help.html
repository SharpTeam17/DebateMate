Help

Log in and join the debate

Click here to log in.

After logging in for the first time, you will become a spectator to the debate.

If you want to join the debate as a debater, click here to join the debate.

You will be shown the debate topic and asked to choose your side.

Create a new argument

To create a new argument, you must first join as a Debater.

You can post a new argument by typing your argument under the "Post an argument" text box on the homepage.

You must include a source (accessible via a URL).

Replying to arguments and adding comments

To add a comment or reply to a posted argument, simply click on the "Comment on this argument" link linked in the argument.

Reporting arguments and comments

To report an argument, simply click on the "Report Argument" link in the argument card.

To report a comment, simply click on the "Report Comment" link in the comments section.

Once reported, a moderator will look at the report and determine the best course of action, which may include removal of comment/ argument or banning of user in question.
