DebateMate Rules

Roles

Spectator

Spectator is the default role in a debate, upon logging in. Spectators are allowed to view all debate arguments and comments. They are also allowed to report comments. Spectators are responsible for judging and voting each argument in the debate. They are responsible for choosing the "winner" based on several criteria.

Debater

Users can choose to become debaters if they wish to participate in the debate: they can add new arguments and add comments. They must choose a side in response to the debate topic. They are not allowed to vote.

Moderator

Moderators are responsible for removing comments/ arguments based on reports, banning users, and generally keeping the debate on topic. Only certain users can be made moderator.

Code of Conduct

Please treat everyone respectfully in a debate.

Profanity is not allowed.

Do not engage in off topic conversations.

Repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban.
